Quality + Hygiene

Quality Policy

Our quality check begins right from the moment we choose our raw material. We carefully select and test our raw materials daily at our in-house lab. The entire process of manufacturing is keenly supervised from pre to post production. Utmost care is taken to ensure the guarantee of quality, hygiene, and taste. We have engineered a unique technique and process to fulfill the demand for high quantity production while maintaining the parameters for taste and quality.

Ingredient Selection

A standardized process has been installed to keep a check on the selection of the ingredients as we believe that the right ingredients are the most crucial part of the manufacturing. Our suppliers have been providing us with their services for decades and are familiar with the quality requirements for the brand. The ingredients are tested in our in-house lab using contemporary technology to give you the tastiest product.

Preservation

Preservation is just as necessary as manufacturing. Both the raw materials and finished products require preservation as it gets distributed from various channels during their shelf life. Our streamlined process helps us with maintaining high levels of product movements on a daily basis. We have adopted some new technologies while maintaining some old tried and tested hard customs to maintain the supply chain of fresh products.

Quality Check

Quality marks the identity of your product and we learned this early on our way into the business, Thus, we have developed a process to keep a tab on the journey of our products. It begins at the testing of our raw materials and goes beyond providing ITC’s ECF(Elemental Chlorine Free) USA FDA standard paper packing for the finished products.
